Follow Three Simple steps to get started!

1. Start saving your passwords by creating a Segment by clicking on the New Segment Tab.

3. To access your password click on the Eye icon and enter the passcode set at the time of Registration. Unless the correct passcode is entered you will be UNABLE to access your saved passwords.
